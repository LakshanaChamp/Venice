YOU'RE GOING TO LOVE VENICE

Great handicraft

The art of handicraft in Venice is as unique as passionate as it was centuries ago. You can savor a Venice of almost another era, discovering the secrets of master craftsmen that will create under your eyes the "pearls" of Venice's top handcraft tradition: velvets, brocades, books, frames, furnishings, leather bindings, Carnival masks and other wonders.
